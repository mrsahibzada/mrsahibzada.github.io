Hajj Crowd Monitoring

  • Duration:Aug 2020 â€“ Present

Huge crowds of people gather in Makkah for Hajj. Both Arafat and muzadalifah lead to Mina and there are possible collision points in Mina. There is about a crowd flow of 2 million people in 2 hours at Mina.
The number of pilgrims and the Hajj deaths due to catastrophes over the past years have increased overtime and no impactful steps have been taken to resolve this issue.
The current monitoring system makes use of cctv cameras, human operators and helicopters, but it has a lot of problems i.e. Crowd collisions, Death, overcrowding and traffic jams. By using only have the cctv footage, we got the frames, applied a mask on it and used it to calculate the respective Crowd Count and Crowd Flow which will be used for further processing to approach the solution.
We form clusters and we get the velocity of the crowd in 8 directions .
This project could possibly mean that there would be a decrease in the number of catastrophes happening during the time of the Hajj and would possibly allow the Saudi Management to identify problems with the current infrastructure and would allow them the locations where and how the infrastructure can be improved.
The project was developed in Python using Numpy, Matplotlib, Pandas, CV2, TensorFlow, PyTorch and concepts of Machine Learning and Computer Vision.
